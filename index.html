At 36 hours during the third edition of Slavic Game Jam 2017 we made the game!

It's a 3D game and made in HTML5 and Javascript.

The story is based on main topic this year SGJ'17 - UNKNOW. It's realy simple - you just wake up as a monkey on crashed spaceship and you have to get out. But the door have a specific code and there is only one good choice...
